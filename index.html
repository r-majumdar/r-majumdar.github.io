Rajeshwari Majumdar

Welcome! I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Politics at New York University.

My research interests lie in political behavior, experimental methods, and Indian politics. In my dissertation, I examine how group identity affects reactions to information about contentious political issues, and develop ways to reduce intergroup prejudice and support for religious nationalism.
